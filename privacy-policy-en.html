Privacy Policy - Colaí App

Last updated: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Colaí App handles user information.

Colaí App helps users organize sticker and card collections, track owned, missing, and duplicate items, and optionally share or scan collection lists.

1. Information the app does not collect directly

Colaí App does not include:

  • user registration;
  • login or account creation;
  • chat between users;
  • an internal messaging system;
  • direct collection of name, email, phone number, or address;
  • payments outside official app stores.

The app does not ask users to submit personal information to use its main features.

2. Purchases and premium subscription

The app may offer an optional purchase or subscription to unlock extra features, such as removing ads, sharing lists, scanning/importing lists, and other premium features related to collection management.

These purchases are processed by official app stores, such as Google Play and the Apple App Store. Colaí App does not directly collect, store, or process credit card, banking, or complete payment information.

Subscription or purchase information may be processed by third-party services used to validate premium access, such as app stores and subscription management platforms.

3. Ads

The app may display ads provided by third-party advertising networks.

These networks may collect device information, advertising identifiers, usage data, and other information needed to show, measure, and improve ads according to their own privacy policies.

Colaí App does not individually choose each ad shown and does not directly control the content, product, service, company, or advertiser displayed in ad campaigns. Responsibility for the advertised content, offer, product, or service belongs to the advertiser and/or the advertising network that serves the ad.

4. Data collected by third-party services

Although the app does not directly collect personal data through login or registration, some integrated services may collect technical or operational data, such as:

  • device identifiers;
  • advertising identifiers;
  • purchase or subscription information;
  • app usage data;
  • performance data;
  • crash and diagnostic logs;
  • information needed to show ads or validate premium features.

These services may include:

  • Google Play;
  • Google AdMob or another advertising network;
  • RevenueCat or an equivalent subscription management service;
  • diagnostic, performance, or stability services, if used by the app.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy and terms of use.

5. List sharing

The app may allow users to share lists of missing or duplicate stickers/cards.

Sharing is initiated by the user and may happen through external apps installed on the device, such as messaging apps, email, or other operating-system sharing options.

Colaí App does not include internal chat and does not intermediate conversations between users.

6. Children and teenagers

The app is not specifically directed to young children and does not request registration, login, or submission of personal information.

We recommend that minors use the app with guidance from a parent or guardian, especially regarding ads, purchases, subscriptions, and sharing information.

Where supported by the platform, the app may request an age range signal from the app store or operating system to provide age-appropriate access to purchases, ads, and premium features.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the app's operation and reduce risks related to misuse of information.

However, no system, app, or data transmission is completely secure. Users should use the app responsibly and avoid sharing personal information in lists, notes, or exported content.

8. Data retention

Because the app does not include its own account system, we do not maintain an account associated with the user.

Data related to purchases, subscriptions, ads, diagnostics, or app operation may be retained by third-party services according to their own retention policies.

Data stored locally on the device, such as collection lists and item information, may remain on the device until the user deletes the app data or uninstalls the app.

9. Device permissions

The app may request permissions only when needed for a specific feature.

If a permission is requested, the user may accept or decline it through the operating system. Some features may not work correctly if certain permissions are denied.

10. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the app, services used, or legal and app-store requirements.

The latest update date will always be shown at the beginning of this document.
